Convert Airtime to Money

Easily convert your airtime into cash with our seamless process.

Please note that a 15% fee will be deducted from the amount you send. The remaining balance will be credited to your account.

Steps to Convert Airtime to Money

  1. Send Money:

    Transfer the specified amount to this number: xxx-xxxx.

  2. Fill Out the Form:

    After completing the transfer, fill out the form with the following details:

    • Your name (Required)
    • Phone number (Required)
    • Email Address (Optional)
    • Amount transferred (Required)
    • Date of Transfer (Required)
    • Account Number (if not provided will transfer it to the provided phone number)
    • Screenshot of payment for fast processing (Optional)
  3. Confirmation:

    We will verify your transfer and form submission.

  4. Account Credit:

    Once confirmed, your account / phone number will be credited immediately with the remaining amount after the 15% fee.
