How to Pay Electricity Bills for AEDC, BEDC, EEDC, EKEDC, IBEDC, IKEDC, JED, KAEDCO, KEDCO & PHED Online in Nigeria

  1. Choose your electricity company/disco (AEDC, BEDC, EEDC, EKEDC, IBEDC, IKEDC, JED, KAEDCO, KEDCO, or PHED).
  2. Select your meter type. It can be either Prepaid (if you load tokens into your meter) or Postpaid.
  3. Enter your meter number and crosscheck it to ensure that it's correct.
  4. Enter the amount of electricity that you wish to purchase. There is a minimum limit of ₦1,000 and a maximum limit of ₦100,000 per transaction.
  5. Click on the "Continue" button to validate your meter/account number. If the meter number is valid, you will be redirected to the checkout page where the customer name and address are shown for your review.
  6. If everything is right, kindly click on the "Buy Now" button on the checkout page to make a payment.
  7. You can choose to pay with your wallet funds/balance, card, or bank transfer.
  8. After a successful payment, you will receive your electricity unit token via SMS, Email, API response, and the Order page.
